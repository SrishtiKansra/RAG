Artist Profile: Luna Vega

Luna Vega is a classical-electronic crossover musician originally from Lisbon,
Portugal. She is best known for playing the cello, which she blends with
ambient synthesizers in her live performances.

Luna Vega rose to prominence with her debut album "Tidal Glass" in 2014. She
co-founded the experimental band Quantum Echo in 2011, which she still tours
with today. Critics often describe her sound as a fusion of baroque melody and
modern electronica.
